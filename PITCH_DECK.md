# Kelper: Kenyan Equity Legal Framework
**Pitch Deck**

## 1. The Problem
Imported templates (like the US SAFE) fail Kenyan founders. They rely on concepts ("authorized shares", "capital stock") that are legally meaningless under the Kenyan Companies Act, 2015. 
Moreover, traditional contracts rely on courts for enforcement, but in Kenya, commercial litigation can take years and cost more than the original investment. Startups need protection mechanisms that work in the *real* East African environment, including anti-siphoning guardrails and fast dispute resolution.

## 2. The Solution: Kelper (The Cross-Border Legal Bridge)
Kelper is an automated, AI-powered document generator that creates plain-language, court-alternative agreements built specifically for Kenya, **while bridging international standards.** Unlike any competitor in the market, Kelper explicitly anchors all clauses in both the **Constitution of Kenya (e.g. Article 40)** and the **Kenyan Companies Act, 2015**, while structurally mirroring the **US Delaware General Corporation Law (DGCL)** and **International Chamber of Commerce (ICC) standards**.
* **Cross-Border Compatibility**: Foreign VCs (especially in the US) can invest seamlessly because our documents are legally mapped to Delaware standards.
* **Kenyan Companies Act, 2015 compliant**: Uses exact legal concepts like share allotment, Form CR2, and stamp duty.
* **Built-in Guardrails**: Founder salary caps, M-Pesa dual-signatory thresholds, and personal fraud guarantees.
* **International Med-Arb**: Fast-track arbitration under UNCITRAL rules, enforceable globally via the New York Convention.
* **Plain English**: Every clause includes an automated plain-language translation.

## 3. Product Demonstration
Kelper offers a **"Real Docs" UI**:
* Founders fill in their details on a simple form.
* A realistic, editable legal document generates in real-time.
* Users can click any clause to see its "Plain English" translation and "Kenyan Law Context".
* **Groq AI Integration**: Users can chat directly with a specialized AI legal assistant. The AI reads the specific clause the user clicked and answers their legal questions instantly, grounded in Kenyan corporate law.

## 4. Why Now?
The Kenyan tech ecosystem is booming (Silicon Savannah), attracting millions in early-stage venture capital. Yet, early-stage legal infrastructure remains stuck in the past. Founders are signing 60-page imported documents they don't understand, leading to disastrous cap-table mistakes and unresolvable founder disputes. Kelper modernizes this infrastructure today.
